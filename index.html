About Me

Last updated 19 June by Wenli Zhang

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Management Information Systems in the Eller College of Management at the University of Arizona. I am working as a research assistant in the INSITE Center under the direction of Dr. Sudha Ram.

My research interests are in the area of Machine Learning and Text Mining, specifically in developing techniques based on Natural Language Processing, Network Analysis, and large scale distributed computing​ for ​solving real world problems and applying these to healthcare challenges. Specifically, my dissertation work focuses on Using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning for Chronic Disease Management and Prevention. My paper has been published in journals such as IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics. My research has been presented at numerous international meetings such as Workshop on Information Technologies and Systems (WITS), Workshop on Information Systems Economics (WISE), INFORMS Annual Meeting, International Conference on Digital Health Conference (DigitalHealth), Workshop on Health IT and Economics (WHITE), etc.
